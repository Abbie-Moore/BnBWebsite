Local Area

Our Bed and Breakfast is located in a lovely coastal village. It is a short walk from the beach and the local shops and facilities. There are hiking trails leading to stunning views of the village and magnificent waterfalls in the area. While there is lots to do all around you while you are here, it is still far enough away in a calm, secluded area to give you peace and quiet and a relaxing time away.
