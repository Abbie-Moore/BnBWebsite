Dundee Room

This is our Dundee Room! This twin room lets you enjoy the beautiful sunrise every morning with its large glass doors leading onto the small balocny.

The room includes a TV, giving you acess to a number of streaming websites. The room also includes a mini fridge and a place to make teas and coffees (although please still free to use the shared kitchen donw the hall!).

The sheets are always freshly fitted before your arrival to ensure your comfort and of course there are extra pillows and blankets in the room. This room does not have an ensuite, however there are two more large communal bathrooms accessable just down the hall.
