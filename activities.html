Things To Do!

Our Bed and Breakfast is the perfect place for you to relax and unwind. We offer a range of packages depending on what you want to do.


If you enjoy the outdoors we offer guided hikes where you can learn all about nature and explore the beauty of the area.


If getting hiking isn't for you then we also offer a relaxing day at the spa where you can sit back, relax and let your worries melt away all while keeping the kids entertained at the pool.



Whatever your idea of relaxing is, we have it covered.


Back at the house we have four beautiful rooms, each with their own features and two include an ensuite, a spacious shared kitchen and two shared bathrooms. We are pet friendly and have a shed full of garden toys and games for the children.
