Aberdeen Room

This is our Aberdeen Room! This room is designed to feel like a home away from home. In the mornings the room fills with natural sunlight making it warm and cozy.

The room includes a TV, giving you acess to a number of streaming websites. The room also includes a mini fridge and a place to make teas and coffees (although please still free to use the shared kitchen donw the hall!).

The sheets are always freshly fitted before your arrival to ensure your comfort and of course there are extra pillows and blankets in the room.
